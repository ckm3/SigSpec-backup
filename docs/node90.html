phdist:phases <int> ()

generates a Phase Distribution Diagram for the sampling of the given time series, p.. By default, no Phase Distribution Diagram is computed.

parameter: number of phase angles in the interval , if the maximum probability density is . Between and , twice this number is used, and so on. This enhances the visibility of the Phase Distribution Diagram also in frequency and phase regions associated with a very eccentric phase distribution.
