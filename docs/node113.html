sock:phases <int> ()

generates a Sock Diagram for the sampling of the given time series, p.. By default, no Sock Diagram is computed.

parameter: number of phase angles in the interval , if the maximum sock significance is . Between and , twice this number is used, and so on. This enhances the visibility of the Sock Diagram also in frequency and phase regions associated with a high sock significance.
